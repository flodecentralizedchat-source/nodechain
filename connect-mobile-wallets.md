On mobile you must open the site from inside the wallet app:
MetaMask Mobile:

Open the MetaMask app
Tap the menu (bottom tab) → Browser
Type nodechain-silk.vercel.app in the address bar
Tap Connect

Trust Wallet:

Open Trust Wallet app
Tap Browser (bottom nav) → dApps
Type nodechain-silk.vercel.app
Tap Connect

Coinbase Wallet:

Open Coinbase Wallet app
Tap Browser tab
Type nodechain-silk.vercel.app
Tap Connect
